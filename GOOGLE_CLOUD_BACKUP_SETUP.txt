SmartPay India Render Backend - Google Cloud Backup Setup

Required Render Environment Variables:
DATABASE_URL = Render PostgreSQL internal URL
GCS_BUCKET_NAME = smartpay-backup-service
GOOGLE_APPLICATION_CREDENTIALS = /etc/secrets/google-service-account.json
BACKUP_ENCRYPTION_KEY = your private long key

Required Render Secret File:
Filename: google-service-account.json
Contents: paste Google service account JSON key

Backend endpoints added:
POST /api/backup/upload
POST /api/backup/latest
POST /api/backup/download

Do not upload Google JSON key to GitHub.
Do not change BACKUP_ENCRYPTION_KEY after backups are created, otherwise old backups may not restore.
